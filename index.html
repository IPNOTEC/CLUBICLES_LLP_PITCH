Effective Take Rate

Space onboarding: ₹10,001/yr = ~₹833/mo effective. Daily fee at ₹101/day × 25 active days = ₹2,525/mo. Combined platform take on a 10-seat space averaging ₹300 occupancy/day = effective 25–30% platform share — above Airbnb (14–16%) and OYO (15–20%), justified by supply-side SaaS value.

Zero CAPEX

No real estate owned. Pure marketplace + SaaS. Gross margins 70%+ at scale. No lease obligations at any stage.

2-Sided Network Effect

More spaces → more users → more demand → more spaces. First-mover in each neighbourhood = permanent moat.

📌 Why this is healthyVolume & space-side SaaS carries model
Why 4.8:1 is realistic & strong: The ₹10 user booking fee is intentionally low-friction to drive volume. Platform revenue is primarily earned on the supply side (space SaaS). Together, supply + demand economics produce a blended unit model that mirrors Zomato's early growth — low per-transaction user fee, high recurring SaaS from the supply side. Group bookings (4 members × ₹1,001/day) are a volume and utilisation play, not LTV-optimised.

Why 12:1 (Plan A) is realistic & compelling: Space owner chooses one plan — not both. Plan A (₹10,001/yr flat) is simple, low-friction, and predictable. Plan B (₹101/day) suits seasonal or part-time spaces. A 12:1 LTV:CAC on Plan A is strong for a B2B SaaS play — industry benchmark is 3:1 to 5:1. The model gets stronger as spaces stay active longer (Year 2, 3 renewals = near-zero marginal CAC).
📌 How to read these numbers: Space onboarding assumes a 50/50 split — 50 spaces on Plan A (₹10,001/yr) and 50 on Plan B (₹101/day). Daily platform fees shown are Plan B spaces only (50 × ₹101 × 300 active days). Booking fees = 1,000 users × 8 sessions/month × ₹10 × 12 months. These are conservative mid-scenario projections. The model scales when space count grows — each additional space on Plan B adds ~₹30,300/yr with near-zero marginal cost.

Neighbourhood density moat

Local supply density is hyper-defensible. First-mover in each neighbourhood pins the map. No incumbent has any incentive to serve a 500-seat catchment area.

Community switching cost

VIBGYOR persona network creates real switching costs. Users don't leave their professional community. Booking app → platform.

Data flywheel

Every booking generates demand heatmap data. More bookings → better AI → better spaces → more users. Data layer inaccessible to late entrants.
